# Decision Log

> This directory contains records of major decisions and their reasoning.

## Purpose

Decisions shape the trajectory of life and projects. This log preserves:
- **Context**: What situation prompted the decision
- **Options**: What alternatives were considered
- **Reasoning**: Why this choice was made
- **Outcome**: What actually happened (added later)

## Format

Each decision is a separate Markdown file:

```
decisions/
├── README.md (this file)
├── 2026-01-18_starting-pos.md
├── 2026-02-15_choosing-university.md
└── ...
```

## Template

```markdown
---
id: uuid
date: YYYY-MM-DD
title: Decision Title
category: career | learning | project | personal | system
status: made | in-progress | revisited
---

# [Decision Title]

## Context
What situation required this decision?

## Options Considered
1. Option A — pros/cons
2. Option B — pros/cons
3. Option C — pros/cons

## Decision
What was chosen and why?

## Expected Outcome
What do you expect to happen?

---

## Actual Outcome (Added Later)
What actually happened? Was this the right call?

## Lessons
What would you do differently?
```
